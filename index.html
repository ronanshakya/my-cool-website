Cook Diary Entry

As a spoiled brat from Kathmandu, Nepal, I never thought I'd find myself in a kitchen, let alone in Canada, all alone. Back home, I was accustomed to having my mum prepare exquisite meals. But here in Canada, I didn't have my mum to rely on anymore. With reluctance, I started my journey of self-discovery in the world of cooking. Armed with YouTube tutorials, and video calls with my mum, and a newfound appreciation for independence, I began experimenting in the kitchen. On thing I've learned so far is that cooking is difficult, especially when you have to do it three times a day. I have a newfound appreciation for my mum.
